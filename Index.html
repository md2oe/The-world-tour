🪐 Planets of the Solar System

☀️ Sun

The center of our solar system. A giant ball of fire and energy! 🔥

🟤 Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun. It has no atmosphere and is very hot during the day! 🌡️

🟠 Venus

Hottest planet due to its thick atmosphere. Also known as Earth's twin! ♀️🔥

🌍 Earth

Our home! The only planet known to support life. 🌊🌳👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

🔴 Mars

The red planet. Scientists are exploring it for signs of life. 🛸🔬

🟡 Jupiter

The biggest planet! Famous for its giant red storm and many moons. 🌪️🌕

🔵 Saturn

Known for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock. 💍🧊

💙 Uranus

Spins on its side and has a bluish color due to methane gas. 🌀❄️

💜 Neptune

Farthest planet from the Sun. Very cold and windy! 🌬️🌊

🇮🇳 India’s Freedom Fight (1857–1947)

🕊️ India was under British rule for over 200 years (1757–1947).

🔥 Movements like Non-Cooperation (1920), Civil Disobedience (1930) & Quit India (1942) were led by Gandhiji.

🧑‍🏫 Key leaders: Mahatma Gandhi, Subhas Chandra Bose, Bhagat Singh, Jawaharlal Nehru, Sardar Patel, and many more.

📜 After long struggle, India became independent on 15th August 1947.

⚔️ India–Pakistan Wars

🇮🇳 India and 🇵🇰 Pakistan have fought several wars since Partition in 1947:

🛡️ 1947–48 War: After partition, over Kashmir. India defended successfully.

🛡️ 1965 War: Pakistan attacked Kashmir again. Indian Army gave strong reply.

🛡️ 1971 War: India helped East Pakistan (now 🇧🇩 Bangladesh) gain freedom. Huge victory for India.

🛡️ 1999 Kargil War: Pakistan soldiers crossed into Indian land. Brave Indian soldiers won it back. 🇮🇳 Jai Hind!

🎖️ Many brave soldiers sacrificed their lives for the country. We salute them. 🙏

🗓️ Indian History Timeline

🛕 Ancient India: 3300 BCE – Indus Valley Civilization (Harappa, Mohenjodaro)

📜 Vedic Period: 1500 BCE – Early Hindu scriptures & rituals written

🏛️ Maurya Empire: 322 BCE – Emperor Ashoka spread Buddhism

🌟 Gupta Empire: 320 CE – Golden Age of science, maths & art

🕌 Delhi Sultanate & Mughals: 1206–1707 – Muslim rule, major architecture like Red Fort, Taj Mahal

🧭 British Rule: 1757–1947 – Colonial exploitation begins with Battle of Plassey

🇮🇳 Independence: 1947 – India becomes a free nation

⚔️ Indo-China War (1962)

🌄 China attacked Indian territory in Ladakh and Arunachal in 1962.

😢 India was unprepared and suffered loss, but soldiers fought bravely.

🇮🇳 Today, India is better prepared with advanced tech and strong defense.

💔 Pulwama Attack (2019)

🚌 On 14 February 2019, 40 CRPF soldiers were killed in a terrorist attack in Pulwama, J&K.

😡 This attack shocked the whole country and led to a strong response.

🎯 Surgical Strike (2016)

🗡️ After Uri terror attack, Indian Army conducted a surgical strike on terrorist camps across LoC in Pakistan on 29 Sept 2016.

💪 It proved India's ability to respond smartly and strongly.

🦸‍♂️ Great Freedom Fighters of India

Chapter 3: Atoms and Molecules

  • Atoms are the smallest unit of matter.
  • Molecules are groups of atoms; chemical formulas show ratios.
  • Law of conservation of mass and constant proportion apply.

Atomic mass unit (u), Mole concept, Avogadro's number: 6.022×10²³
